Got this from Wired, a rather old story:

Rob Malda, better known as CmdrTaco, editor and founder of Slashdot, posted a marriage proposal to his long-time girlfriend, Kathleen Fent, on the front page of the techie website Thursday morning.

Fifteen minutes later, Malda received an e-mail reply. The subject read "Yes." The message was "Dork. You made me cry. :)"

Others posted messages urging Kathleen to accept. "You know he loves you because he spell-checked this post," in a reference to Malda's notoriously creative spelling skills.

It was a happy ending.
