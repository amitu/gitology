After much reluctence I have started using Google Desktop, and after so long I have started reading news! Bloglines is one thing, I used to subscribe to feeds from regular news sites hoping to read them, but the volume used to be too high to keep up with. Google Desktop has a peculiar TV like news display, latest 10-12 news items keep scrolling.

Another thing that stops me from using the autohide feature is the Photos widget. Its a pleasure to see the photos from old times scrolling every so often. A minimal but functional ToDo pane is also very useful.

Incidentally I have almost never used its search, Ctrl-E/firefox search box is still my preferred way of searching. Google also acknowledges this by renaming Google Desktop Search to Google Desktop.
