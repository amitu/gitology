Geek Girl and I would like to present you smileyfy: a greasemonkey userscript for Firefox. It converts all the ascii art smileys to their gif/image representations. To use it you will need Firefox and you will have to have greasemonkey extension installed on it. You may try your luck with GreaseMonkIE too. Here is smileyfy userscript, open it in browser, and then click on "Install User Script" from the Tools menu. Enjoy! B-)
