Mozilla foundation strikes on Microsoft again, this time with an email application, reports c|net. But this time they are entering a market with no dearth of mature products, and some would even wonder if with the advent of such cool webmails like GMail, the market itself has a future. Read the why document, "Why You Should Use the Mozilla Thunderbird E-mail Program?", to get a quick overview of the features it includes. The best feature ofcourse is the meta feature, called extensions, that lets you install new features if something is missing.

But that is not what is going to change the world, take a look at the Mozilla Amazon Browser (requires Mozilla/Firefox, click on the link and see the magic!), it can.
